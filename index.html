ANALISA

With the entrance of COVID19 into the business life, a lot of companies switched to online interviews from face-to-face interviews. In companies, human resources managers may want to inspect the processes of the recruitments. Additionally, interviewers also may choose to have a real time detailed character analysis displayed on screen to better evaluate the candidates. To do this, we are planning to develop an application which is specifically used for online job interviews. In our application, the interviewee’s facial expression and gestures will be analyzed to determine the interviewee’s personality, current mood, level of anxiety and self-confidence. The interviewee side will not see anything other than a normal video conference app while the interviewer side sees the interviewee related info and an AI based analysis of his/her actions instantaneously besides the video call. Video chat part of the application will be implemented as a web app, whereas the analysis part of it will be on the interviewee’s local machine as a desktop application.
